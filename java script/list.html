how to cook jellof rice

  1. boil rice
  2. season the meat
  3. cook the tomotoes
  4. wash the rice
  5. add the cooked tomatoes
  6. add the meat
  7. add jellof rice spices
  8. cook till ready and serve

how to cook indomie

  • boil the indomie
  • after five minutes add the spices
  • add cooked meat or fish
  • stir for five minutes and serve
